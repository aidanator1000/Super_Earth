The Galaxy

Seyshel Beach

One of the prettiest planets to visit, Seyshel Beach is full of both glorious beachfronts and luxurious greenery, bearing soft grey sand, pink plants, and cotton-candy sky.

Oasis

Full of lush greenery and bright sun, Oasis is considered the ideal tropical getaway (although there is no reason to get away from Super Earth.) Patrolling Helldivers now routinely check for Illuminate breaches.

New Haven

With a beautiful night time walk, New Haven is considered a perfect place for new couples to enjoy romantic outdoor scenery. Protected from anti democratic threats with an orbital laser 24/7, rest assured your visit will be perfect!

Malevelon Creek

A landmark of the efforts from the Helldivers against the Automaton threat. One of 5 sectors of the planet is dedicated to the burial grounds of all deployed soldiers. The planet, despite being currently liberated, is restricted due to Automaton proximity.

Angels Venture

- - UPDATE - -

The Meridia Singularity has collided with Angels Venture, resulting in the tragic loss of 454,427,844 voters. May they rest in freedom, and all routes in collision course have been closed.

Fori Prime

- - WARNING - -

Fori Prime, currently consumed by the Gloom, is the only affected planet to be traversable. Unless using new filtration technology, anyone who breathes in Gloom spores is rendered incapacitated.

Meridia Singularity

- - WARNING - -

In response to the developed Terminid supercolony, dark energy bombs were used to collapse the planet. This led to the Singularity, acting as a gateway for the Illuminate to return. The Singularity is now on a course towards Super Earth.
